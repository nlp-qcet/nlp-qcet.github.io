QGO-c-1-1 : Absence of unnecessary repetition (content)

Definition:
The degree to which the content of an output is free of unnecessarily repeated elements.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is the content of this output free of unnecessarily repeated elements, ignoring its form?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs has fewer unnecessarily repeated elements in its conent/meaning, ignoring its form?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "Is the Text free from unnecessary repetition of content?"
