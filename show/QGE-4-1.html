Node Details
QGE-4-1 : Referent resolvability

Definition:
The degree to which the referents of the referring expressions in an output can be identified.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree can the referent of the referring expressions in this output be identified?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs has referring expressions make it easier to identify the referent?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "how easily can you find the object in the image based on the phrase given?", "how easy is it to identify who the referring expressions are referring to?"
