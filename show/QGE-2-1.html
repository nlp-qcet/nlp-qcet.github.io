Node Details
QGE-2-1 : Humanlikeness

Definition:
The degree to which an output could have been produced by a human.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree could this output have been produced by a human?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs is more likely to have been produced by a human?
Additional notes and information:
The degree to which an output could have been produced by a human. Can be evaluated e.g. via assessing similarity with human-authored text.
