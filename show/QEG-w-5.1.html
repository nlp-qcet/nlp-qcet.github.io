Node Details
QEG-w-5.1 : Ease of Communication

Definition:
A better system produces outputs that make communication with the user easier
Attestations:
In their evaluation of ease of communication with a new dialogue system, Rieser et al., 2011 ask evaluators for a rating on a scale from 1 to 6 for how well users perceived they were understood by the system.
Additional notes and information:
[QEG-w-5.1] Ease of Communication captures the perceived or measured ease with which users (i) convey their communicative goals to the system, and (ii) understand what the system tells them.
