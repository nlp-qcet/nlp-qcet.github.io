QFE-2 : Inferrability of speaker/author stance

Definition:
The degree to which the speaker’s/author’s stance towards an OBJECT is inferrable from the text. Stance is always positive/negative (with possible intermediate value(s)), but might be phrased differently, e.g.supports/doesn't support, is friendly towards/is hostile towards, etc. Open-class criterion.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] n/a
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] n/a

Additional notes and information:
E.g. "rank these texts in order of positivity expressed towards the company?", "is the text making fun of the government/the president?"
