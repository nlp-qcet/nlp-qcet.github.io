Node Details
QOG-w-4 : Humanlikeness

Definition:
A better system produces outputs that are more human-like
Elicitation:
Intrinsic, subjective, relative: Which of these outputs is more human-like?
Intrinsic, subjective, absolute: To what degree is this output human-like?
Attestations:
Cercas Curry et al., 2015 assess the humanlikeness of navigation instructions by asking evaluators to rate the extent to which an instruction ``could have been produced by a human'' on a 4-point scale.
