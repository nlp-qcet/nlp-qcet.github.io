QGO-b-3-1-1 : Speed of Understanding

Definition:
The degree to which the meaning of an output can be quickly understood.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is this output quick to understand?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs is more quickly understood?
Additional notes and information:
The speed with which the meaning of an output is absorbed, i.e., is fast to understand as well as possible and easy to understand.
