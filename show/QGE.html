QG : Goodness of outputs (excluding correctness)

Node Details
QGE : Goodness of outputs relative to an external frame of reference

Definition:
The degree to which an output is good relative to a system-external frame of reference.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is this output good relative to a system-external frame of reference?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs is better relative to a system-external frame of reference?
Additional notes and information:
None.
