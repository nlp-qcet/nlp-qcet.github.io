QCE-c : Correctness of outputs relative to external frame of reference (content)

Definition:
The degree to which the content of a text is correct relative to a system-external frame of reference.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is the content of this text correct relative to a system-external frame of reference?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these texts has the more correct content relative to a system-external frame of reference?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "are the contents of the text factually true?" (best = no untrue facts)
