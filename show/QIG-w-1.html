Node Details
QIG-w-1 : Parse Accuracy (reference-less)

Definition:
A better system produces outputs that are more complete and accurate parses of the input
Elicitation:
Intrinsic, subjective, relative: Which of these outputs is a more complete and accurate parse of the input?
Intrinsic, subjective, absolute: To what degree is this output a complete and accurate parse of the input?
Attestations:
Opitz & Frank, 2022 evaluate parsers by asking evaluators to asess the `parse acceptability' of parser outputs (graphs) given the input sentence.
Additional notes and information:
Parse Accuracy is sometimes assessed by asking parsing experts whether e.g. output phrase-structure or depency parses represent complete and correct analyses of the input sequence.
