QGO-b : Goodness of outputs in their own right (both form and content)

Definition:
The degree to which the form and content/meaning of an output is of good quality, considering only the output.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is the form and content/meaning of this output of good quality, considering only the output?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs has the better form and content/meaning, considering only the outputs?
Additional notes and information:
None.
