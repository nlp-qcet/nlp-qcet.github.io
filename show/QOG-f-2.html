QOG-f-2 : Speech Quality

Definition:
A better system produces speech that is of better quality
Elicitation:
Intrinsic, subjective, relative: Which of these speech samples is of better quality overall?
Intrinsic, subjective, absolute: To what degree is this text free of pronunciation errors?
Attestations:
Hu & Loizou, 2007 ask evaluators to rate speech enhancement outputs in terms of the level of distortion of the speech signal on a 5-point scale ranging from "very natural, no degradation" to "very unnatural, very degraded."
