QGE-3-4 : Ease of communication

Definition:
The degree to which the outputs make communication easy, typically in a dialogue situation.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] How easy is it to communicate with the system?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of the versions of this system is easier to communicate with?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "how smoothly did the conversation go with the virtual agent?"
