Node Details
QIF-w-2 : Control over Sentiment

Definition:
A better system produces outputs that are more at the target level of positivity/negativity provided in the input
Attestations:
Kumar et al., 2024 assess the effectiveness of sentiment control (negative, positive, uncontrolled) in a feedback generator as the accuracy according to majority voting by four sentiment classifiers.
Additional notes and information:
[QIF-w-2] Control over Positive/Negative Sentiment captures, usually at the whole-text level, the overall tone of a text reflecting positive/negative disposition either to its topic(s) overall, or towards a specific aspect. This QC is for systems where target levels of positivity/negativity are defined (commonly positive, neutral, negative) and the system is trained to achieve them (possibly among other things including in LLMs).
