QGE-1-2-f : Appropriateness (form)

Definition:
The degree to which the form of the output is appropriate in the given context/situation.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is the form of this output appropriate in this context/situation?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs has the more appropriate form in this context/situation?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "are the lexical choices appropriate given the target reader (expert VS layperson, adult VS child, etc.)?"
