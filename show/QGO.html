QGO : Goodness of outputs in their own right

Definition:
The degree to which an output is of good quality, looking only at the output.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is this output of good quality, considering only the output?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs is better, considering only the outputs?

None.
