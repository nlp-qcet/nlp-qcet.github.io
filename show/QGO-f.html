QGO-f : Goodness of outputs in their own right (form)

Definition:
The degree to which the form of an output is of good quality, considering only the output.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is the form of this output of good quality, considering only the output?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs has the better form, considering only the outputs?
Additional notes and information:
E.g. "is the generated response a complete sentence (e.g. “it is in Indiana”) and not a sentence fragment (e.g. “Indiana”)?"
