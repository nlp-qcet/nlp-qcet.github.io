QGE-1-2 : Appropriateness

Definition:
The degree to which the output is appropriate in the given context/situation.
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, absolute mode:
[generic] To what degree is this output appropriate in this context/situation?
Suggested question to evaluators in subjective, intrinsic, relative mode:
[generic] Which of these outputs is more appropriate in this context/situation?
Additional notes and information:
None.
