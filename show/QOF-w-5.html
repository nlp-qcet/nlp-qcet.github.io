QOF-w-5 : Humorousness/Non-humorousness

Definition:
A better system produces outputs that are either (a) more humorous, or (b) less humorous
Elicitation:
Intrinsic, subjective, relative: Which of these outputs is more humorous?
Intrinsic, subjective, absolute: To what degree is this output humorous?
Attestations:
