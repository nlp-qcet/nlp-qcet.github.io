Attestations:

Additional notes and information:
[QEG-w-3.1] Usefulness for Task/Information Need shares the same characteristics of its parent QC[QEG-w-3] Usefulness (nonspecific), but is more specific, assessing usefulness for a given task, such as following generated instructions to trouble-shoot a malfunctioning app, or for a given information need, e.g. are the accommodation descriptions on a website useful in selecting a holiday rental.
